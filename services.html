Common Enquiries

Services FAQ

Answers to common questions about our individual sessions and corporate programmes.

What is your geographic coverage for events?

Beauty with EVE is based in Kuala Lumpur. All events within the Klang Valley are included at no additional travel cost. We are available for events in Penang, Johor Bahru, and other cities across Malaysia; travel and accommodation will be quoted separately based on your location.

Is the Team Workshop HRDC-claimable?

Yes. Evelynn is a registered HRD Corp trainer. We provide all necessary documentation for your levy claim, including the official invoice, attendance sheets, and certificates of completion for all participants.

What are the lead times for booking?

For individual Signature Draping sessions, we recommend booking 1 week in advance. Corporate activations (Retail Events, Drape Parties) require at least 2 weeks. Team Workshops involving HRDC documentation require a minimum of 3 weeks lead time.

How much does a session cost?

Individual sessions start from RM 450. Retail event activations begin at RM 1,500 per session, and Team Workshops start from RM 3,500. We also offer bespoke Corporate Consultations for event planners starting at RM 500.
