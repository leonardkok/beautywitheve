Colour Analysis Event

JD Sports

Location: Pavilion Kuala Lumpur, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

Date: March 15, 2026

On 15 March 2026, Evelynn Koo brought personal colour analysis to the JD Sports store at Pavilion Kuala Lumpur. Guests received one-on-one consultations to identify their seasonal colour type, with each result translated directly into activewear and footwear colour recommendations from JD Sports' current range.

The session showed how colour science applies beyond fashion and beauty — knowing whether you carry warm, cool, or neutral tones makes a real difference in choosing sportswear that flatters rather than clashes. Each guest left with a Personal Colour Card to guide future purchases both in-store and online.
